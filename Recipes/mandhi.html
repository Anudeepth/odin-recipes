Mandhi

Mandhi is a traditional Omani dish that consists of rice, meat, and spices. It is a flavorful and aromatic meal that is popular in Oman and the broader Gulf region.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or lamb
  • Rice
  • Onion and garlic
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, cardamom)

steps

  1. Cook the meat with onion and garlic until browned.
  2. Add the spices and cook for another minute.
  3. Stir in the rice and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Add water or broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer until the rice is cooked and the flavors are well combined.
