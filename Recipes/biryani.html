Biryani

Biryani is a popular South Asian dish that consists of fragrant rice, meat, and spices. It is a flavorful and aromatic meal that is enjoyed across many cultures in the region.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or lamb
  • Rice
  • Onion and garlic
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, cardamom)

steps

    It is a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

    Ingredients:

    • Ground beef or Italian sausage
    • Onion and garlic
      • Tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes
    • Lasagna noodles

    steps

    1. Cook the meat with onion and garlic until browned.
    2. Add tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes, and simmer for 20 minutes.
    3. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
    4. Layer the meat sauce, noodles, and cheese in a baking dish.
    5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45 minutes or until bubbly and golden on top.
